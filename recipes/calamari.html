Calamari Recipe

Description

Very tasty fried calamari. Goes great with italian dishes

Ingredients

  • 1lb. Calamari Rings
  • 1-1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoobs garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley flakes
  • Lemon Wedges
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • Cooking procedure

Steps

  1. Combine flour,salt,garlic powder, and parsley flakes
  2. Heat cooking oil in a wok or cooking pot
  3. Drain milk of the calamri. Dredge calamri rings on flour mixture
  4. Deep fry the coated calamari rings in hot oil for 2 minutes
  5. Strain the calamari from cooking pot using a skimmer
  6. Transfer the fried calamari
  7. Serve with lemon wedges
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
