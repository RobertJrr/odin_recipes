Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Description

The perfect chicken parm for a date night at home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Flour Mixture
  • Egg Mixture
  • Breadcrumb-Mixture
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Slice chicken for thinner breasts
  2. Use a meat tenderizer to piunt to 1/2 inch thick
  3. Pat dry and season with salt and pepper
  4. Dredge in flour, egg and breadcrumb mixutres
  5. Fry in oil until crispy
  6. Set aside in cooling rack
  7. Transder chicken to baking sheet and top with marinara sauce and mozarella cheese
  8. Bake for 15 minutes
  9. Serve and enjoy!
